I am a PhD Candidate in Finance at Durham University, UK.

My research interests include corporate finance, financial intermediation, financial markets, and applied empirical finance. 

My research combines interdisciplinary methods, including textual analysis, network analysis, machine learning, and AI-based techniques, to study financial markets, firms, and financial institutions.
