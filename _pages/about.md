I am a PhD Candidate in Finance at Durham University.

My research interests include corporate finance, financial intermediation, politics and finance, and financial technology.

My current research combines interdisciplinary methods, including textual analysis, network analysis, machine learning, and AI-based techniques, to study how political, geopolitical, and social shocks shape financial markets, firms, and financial institutions.

I hold two master's degrees from the University of St Andrews (MSc in Money, Banking and Finance) and Durham University (MSc in Economics), with the Best Overall Academic Performance award.
